Mofield Brothers Construction


Founded in 1961 by John and Trigger Mofield, Mofield Brothers Construction Company is now owned by John's son Mike. Mike runs Mofield Brothers with the help of his two sons Martin and Judson Mofield. With over 75 years of experience in the construction industry, they are a trusted, well known company in the Middle Tennessee area.

All three generations have farmed their entire life and have a passion for working the land. They have done it all from laborers, farmers, machine operators, and business leaders. Mofield Brothers is well equipped to handle your next job.
